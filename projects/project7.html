Eat Da Burger App
Burger logger with MySQL, Node, Express, Handlebars and a homemade ORM. Handlebars generated my HTML.
Restaurant app that lets users input the names of burgers they'd like to eat. Whenever a user submits a burger's name, app displays the burger on the left side of the page -- waiting to be devoured. Each burger in the waiting area also has a Devour it! button. When the user clicks it, the burger will move to the right side of the page. Stores every burger in a database, whether devoured or not.
